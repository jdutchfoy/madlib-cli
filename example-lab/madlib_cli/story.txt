I, the sneaky and cunning Tom have stolen Sarah's beautiful sister and plan to steal her precious jewels!

What are a mighty elephant and backpacking hamster to do? Before you can help Sophie, you'll have to collect the shiny diamonds and sparkling rubies that open up the 22 worlds connected to Tom's Lair. There are 5 keys and 8 gems in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
